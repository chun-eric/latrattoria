Our Team

Meet our Celebrated Head Chefs

With a passion for authentic Italian cuisine, La Trattoria was founded by celebrated chefs Enrico Santini and Sofia Bartolini in 2002. Located in the heart of Osaka, we blend fine dining elegance with the warmth of home-cooked meals, using only the freshest, natural ingredients.
